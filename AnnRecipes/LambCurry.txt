Ingredients: 
1 lb ArmShoulder Lamb Chop
6 Cherry red vine tomatoes
2 white onions
1 inch solid chunk ginger
4-5 garlic pods
Spice Mixture - 3 tbsp(heap) of coriander-jeera powder, 1/2 tspn turmeric, 1 tspn red chilly powder, 1 tspn pepper powder (optional), 1/2 tspn garam masala

Directions: 
Heat sunflower oil on low flame for 5 mins. 
Add crushed ginger/garlic. Saute till light brown. 
Add chopped onions. Saute till light brown. Add salt for faster cooking. 
Add tomato. Saute till cooked. Add salt for faster cooking. 
Add chopped lamb pieces. Add spice mix. Mix well. Add salt as needed. 
Pressure cook for 5-6 whistles.
